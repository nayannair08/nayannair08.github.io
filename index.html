About

Performance modeling sits at the edge of engineering and intuition — you're trying to reason about systems that don't yet exist, using tools that are always one step behind the hardware they model. It's a discipline that rewards patience and careful thinking.

Outside of work, I'm interested in the craft of technical writing, how complex ideas get communicated clearly, and the occasional deep dive into topics far outside my field.
